About Pocket Tactics

Made by Edward Lu for #1GAM, February 2015.

Controls:

Controls normally vary depending on what phase you're in, but here's the global ones:
M: Mute background music
1-4: Shortcuts for unit actions at the bottom right
ESC: Additional shortcut for the "Cancel" action
Space: Additional shortcut for the "Skip" action
